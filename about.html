OUR STORY

Bliss Gelato is a new leader in premium artisan
ice cream that’s handcrafted one small batch
at a time. We’re committed to creating a
product with unique and delicious flavours
you’ll only find at our shop.

"The exclusivity of flavours and the fact that we’re an all
-natural, no preservative / additive ice cream brand is what sets
us apart. If it’s hard to pronounce, we don’t want it."

Our passion for ice cream shows in everything we do here
and we’re committed to working with local companies with
the same drive as us to source only the best ingredients for
our ice cream.

Bliss is a modern and impactful ice cream
brand; our goal is to build meaningful
relationships with our community and be the
go-to destination for some dope ice cream
that’ll have you running back for more.

INGRIDIENTS

Here at Bliss Gelato, we always use the highest quality ingredients in our ice
cream. That means if we have to order the best Hojicha tea leaves from Kyoto,
Japan, it’s getting done.

"We're a small batch brand so we can focus on
the important things like texture and flavour, delivering a
premium ice cream in every pint"

From our graham crackers, fudge brownies, and cinnamon rolls to the kaya and
blueberry jam; every one of our mix-ins are made in-house, with no
preservatives/additives or food colouring.
